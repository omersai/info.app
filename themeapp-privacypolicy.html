Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

ThemeGo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage data such as viewed content, downloads, and favorites through analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics).

Device Information

We may collect technical information such as device model, operating system version, and app version to ensure compatibility and performance.

Personal Data

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., support requests).

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze app performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Determine popular content

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as Firebase (Google) for analytics and content management. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties. Data is only shared when necessary to operate the service.

5. Data Retention

We retain anonymized data only as long as necessary for analytics and service improvement.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy

ThemeGo is not intended for children under 4. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data.

9. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
