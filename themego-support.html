Your phone is one of the things you look at the most every day. ThemeGo helps you turn your device into a stylish experience that reflects your personality.

Why is my live wallpaper not moving?

You may need to press and hold on the lock screen to activate the live effect. Also ensure Low Power Mode is disabled.

How do I restore my PRO subscription?

Use the “Restore Purchases” button in the Settings section.

Why do widgets need location access?

Weather widgets require location permission to provide accurate information.
